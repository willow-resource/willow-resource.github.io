gathering

virtual memorial services, funeral planning help, & creative ideas for mourning in isolation

virtual gathering

GatheringUs

"GatheringUs is a lasting online space that brings communities together after a death. You can post a free obituary, crowdfund for expenses or a charity, create gatherings & inform your community."

Forever Missed

An online memorial service specifically made to be easy to use & accessible even to those with limited computer skills.

Keeper

This online memorial service is interactive, & includes features such as a guest/tribute book for friends & family to share their memories or the family tree function, which allows you to map out your family history.

Memories

"A Memories page offers an ad-free, private and secure space to safeguard and share memories of the people, places, moments and milestones that matter the most. " This site will ensure that personal information will remain secure.

YourTribute

This site is a database that offers online memorial services, funeral planning information, advanced planning aid, care-giving, & more.

Shiva.com

Information, guidance, & practices on grieving in the Jewish community during COVID-19. Shiva.com also offers best practices for safely sitting shiva.

eCondolence Viewneral

An interactive & collaborative funeral service that virtually allows you to plan the funeral, invite those who will participate in the service, & allow family & friends to attend virtually, communicate condolence requests & more, all with the help of a funeral director.

funerals

Burial information

Information on best practices & precautions from the Order of the Good Death.

Ask a Mortician: Funerals

Caitlin Doughty of OGD discusses funerals in the age of COVID-19 on YouTube.

CDC guidance for tribal communities

Resources from the CDC on funeral & burial services as well as coping & social distancing intended specifically for members of tribal communities in the US.

Funeralocity

"Funeralocity is changing the way people compare funeral homes by providing families an easy way to look at all of the funeral homes in one spot." They will continue to assist families in arranging burial & cremation during this time.

creative mourning ideas

Pia Interlandi: ideas for creative mourning

Creative Rituals for death in the times of COVID-19 shared by Pia Interlandi on YouTube. One great idea is plaster-casting or drawing the hand of your loved one so that people can "hold their hand" even across current social distances.

Dying in Absentia: Inspiration & Tips

Creative mourning ideas from OGD including collaborative playlist-making to honor the deceased's favorite songs or a virtual altar to commemorate them.
