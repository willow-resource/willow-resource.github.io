GriefShare

Groups meet weekly (now online) to "help you face these challenges & move toward rebuilding your life." Sessions are "self-contained" & include video seminars, group discussion, & personal reflection.

Community Grief Center

"The Community Grief Center helps people of all ages grieving a death find connection and healing." They offer a series of specific grief support groups, such as those for parents or widows/widowers.
