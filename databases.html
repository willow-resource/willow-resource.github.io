Community Grief Center

"The Community Grief Center helps people of all ages grieving a death find connection and healing." They also offer workshops & support groups.

National Alliance on Mental Illness: COVID-19 Guide

NAMI is the largest grassroots mental health organization in the US. This is their comprehensive guide to loss during COVID-19. Find you local NAMI here.

Lantern

A public benefit corporation that offers a free checklist to navigate what comes next & guidance for difficult-to-have conversations.

eCondolence: COVID-19 Guide

An organization created to "clarify the questions & offer reliable answers for those who are grieving or who want to help someone who is." Their COVID-19 Guide offers insights into best practices, precautions, & more.

Modern Loss

"A place to share the unspeakably taboo, unbelievably hilarious, & unexpectedly beautiful terrain of navigating your life after a death. Beginners welcome.

Center for Loss & Life Transition

"Led by grief counselor & educator Dr. Alan Wolfelt, we are an organization dedicated to helping people who are grieving & those who care for them."

Ask a Mortician: YouTube Channel

Los Angeles mortician Caitlin Doughty's YouTube channel: "you got death questions, we got death answers."

GriefNet

GriefNet.org is an internet community of persons dealing with grief, death, trauma, & major loss. They offer grief support groups for adults & children.

Grief.com

A database of information founded by David Kessler, author of "Finding Meaning: The Sixth Stage of Grief."

YourTribute

This site is a database that offers online memorial services, funeral planning information, advanced planning aid, care-giving, & more.

OGD COVID-19 Toolkit

"Consider this your one-stop source for reliable & up-to-date facts & guidance on handling death, grief, & end of life planning under COVID-19." (English, Spanish, Vietnamese, Chinese, & Korean)
